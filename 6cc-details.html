What is 6CC?

6CC (6 Case Credits) is a leadership qualification level in Forever Company. It’s a signal that you're actively growing your business, sponsoring team members, and maintaining consistency. With 6CC, you are closer to qualifying for Assistant Manager and other leadership incentives. It's the beginning of international expansion and stronger team building.

Features & Benefits of 6CC:

Leadership Recognition: Be recognized as an active business leader with proven performance.
Team Performance Tracking: Get access to team reports and advanced mentorship tools.
Qualification Base for Manager: Consistent 6CC performance is essential for reaching Assistant Manager and beyond.
Marketing Boost: Eligible for more training, product promotions, and spotlight in company events.
Global Growth Opportunity: Sponsor globally and grow teams across different regions.
Mentorship Role: Guide downline team members to achieve their 4CC or 6CC and grow your income.
